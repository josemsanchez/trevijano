Don Antonio García-Trevijano

The man who explained the concept of Collective Freedom and Formal Democracy

Antonio García-Trevijano collects the title as Favourite Son of his hometown Alhama de Granada

García-Trevijano Relevant Facts:

  • 1927 - Born in Alhama de Granada, Granada, Spain
  • 1960 - Works as attorney in Madrid
  • 1974 - Organises meetings in Paris between Don Juan de Borbón and the republican groups
  • 1974 - Founds 'Democratic Convergence Platform' and 'Platajunta' in the clandestinity to coordinate various pro-democracy parties against the Francoist dictatorship.
  • 1975 - Suffers various arrests and is victim of a serious attack for his work on behalf of political freedom during Franco's lifetime.
  • 2006 - Founds the Citizens' Movement towards the Constitutional Republic of Spain (MCRC)
  • 2008 - Writes 'Atheism Aesthetic'.
  • 2010 - Writes 'A Pure Theory of the Republic' exposing a positive theory of a republican state as a solution to the current political situation in Europe.
  • 2018 - Dies in Madrid, Spain.
